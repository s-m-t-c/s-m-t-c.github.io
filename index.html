SEAN MINHUI TASHI CHUA

Relic of the Disciplines of Geography

I am a multidisciplinary scientist and artist working across Earth observation and data science with a focus on Earth systems, remote sensing, polar regions, sound, and immersive storytelling.

I am a researcher at the Nansen Environmental and Remote Sensing Center in Bergen, Norway, an independent consultant, and an artist.

Some of my current research interests:

  • High-resolution sea ice drift tracking using synthetic aperture radar
  • Acoustic signaling and behavior of Weddell seals (I think I heard the world's best kickdrum when I dropped a hydrophone into the sea ice at McMurdo Sound)
  • Bridging the dark space between modelers and observationalists
  • The "user experience" of interacting with spatial information
  • Hybrid music composition techniques drawing from graphic scores, sonification and ~the vibe~
  • Multi-sensor data integration, combining laser altimetry and microwave radiometry, expanding those beautiful detailed pulses to larger areas (but at what cost)

If you wish to collaborate on something or have any questions, please get in touch.
